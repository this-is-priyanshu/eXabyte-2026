eXathon

Rules

  • Team of 3; idea submission and model development in Phase 1.
  • Presentation required; plagiarism strictly prohibited.
  • Top teams advance to on-campus finals with improvements and judging.

Brainstorm

Rules

  • Team quiz event with technical and general topics.
  • Possible online prelims based on registrations.
  • Final format announced by Quiz Master.

X-Hunt

Rules

  • Team of 4; online prelims followed by on-campus treasure hunt.
  • Solve clues, visit locations, and submit group selfies.
  • Scoring based on accuracy and completion time.

Webbed

Rules

  • Individual front-end web design competition.
  • Dynamic features optional but encouraged.
  • Resources will be provided on event day.

Crypt-X

Rules

  • Individual sequential puzzle challenge.
  • Each answer unlocks the next password-protected question.
  • Judging based on completion time.

Digibit

Rules

  • Team of 2; MCQ-based preliminary round.
  • Qualified teams build a circuit using provided materials.
  • Judging based on efficiency and completion time.

Codeplay

Rules

  • Team of 2 participants.
  • DSA problems with increasing difficulty across rounds.
  • Allowed languages: C, C++, Java.

Cube-X

Rules

  • Individual speed-solving event.
  • Standard cube solving rules apply.
  • Fastest valid solve determines winner.

Verb-O-War

Rules

  • Team of 2 in Oxford-style debate format.
  • One speaker for and one against the motion.
  • Professional conduct mandatory.

eXcelerate

Rules

  • Team mobile gaming competition.
  • Online prelim format.
  • Stable internet required; misconduct leads to disqualification.

Byte Tales

Rules

  • Individual online writing event.
